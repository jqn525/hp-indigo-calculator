Custom Design Guidelines

Design Requirements

For custom printed notepads, please provide:

File Specifications
  • High-resolution files (300 DPI minimum)
  • PDF, AI, or EPS format preferred
  • CMYK color mode for accurate printing
  • Include 0.125" bleed if printing to edges
Design Elements
  • Company logo and branding
  • Contact information (phone, email, website)
  • Custom layouts (forms, calendars, lists)
  • Consider tear-away functionality in design

Note: Our design team can assist with layout optimization and branding placement. Additional design services may apply for complex custom layouts.
