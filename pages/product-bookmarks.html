Bookmark Design Tips
  • Resolution: Use 300 DPI for all images
  • Color Mode: CMYK color mode for accurate printing
  • Bleed: Include 0.125" bleed on all sides
  • Safe Zone: Keep important content 0.25" from edges
  • Text Size: Minimum 8pt for body text, 12pt for headings
  • Orientation: Design with vertical layout in mind
  • Brand Elements: Include logo and contact information clearly
