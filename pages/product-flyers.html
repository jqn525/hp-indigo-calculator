Design Tips for Flyers
  • Resolution: Use 300 DPI for all images
  • Color Mode: CMYK color mode for accurate printing
  • Bleed: Include 0.125" bleed on all sides
  • Safe Zone: Keep important content 0.25" from edges
  • Hierarchy: Use clear visual hierarchy for key information
  • Call-to-Action: Make contact information prominent and easy to read
